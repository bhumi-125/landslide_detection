About Us

At Landslide Detection, we use advanced technology and data analysis to monitor and predict potential landslides. Our mission is to keep you safe and informed. Uttarakhand's breathtaking landscapes are marred by the looming threat of landslides, which can have devastating impacts on communities, infrastructure, and ecosystems. By harnessing the power of predictive modeling and early warning systems, researchers and authorities strive to enhance preparedness and safeguard the lives and livelihoods of those dwelling in the embrace of Uttarakhand's awe-inspiring mountains. We're looking for a solution by creating an Android and web-based application that serves as a platform for efficiently monitoring and managing landslide risks, providing real-time alerts, and facilitating communication and collaboration among authorities, researchers, and the public to enhance landslide preparedness and response.
